Personal Statement

I am a Ph.D Candidate who is interested in meta learning and few-sho learning, believing that technology will enhance the human life and economy. Artificial Intelligence wins a lot of attentions and investments.

Data is a kind of valued strategic resources in the network era, yet mastering the data is insufficient. Instead, only with good command of data mining ability and intelligent algorithm analysis can I acquire the useful information inside it. Widely applied in business administration, production control, market analysis, engineering design and scientific exploration, data mining and analysis is closely bound up with our life.

I have experienced in person the formidable power data carried on, which will eventually drastically change our life. However, a large number of professionals in this field are in highly demand in China to boost its future development. I wish to enhance my professional knowledge and hone practical skills in the real-world challenges.
