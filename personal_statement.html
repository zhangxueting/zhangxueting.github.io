Personal Statement

I am a third year postgraduate student, majoring in management science and engineering in National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), P. R. China. I will receive my Master degree in 2016.12, and I have already been admitted as a 2016 Ph.D. candidate of NUDT without entrance examination for my outstanding academic performance. I am inquiring an available abroad position as a new Ph. D for 09/2017 entry. Furthermore, I have got an opportunity for application of the “Chinese Top University Graduate Students Studying Abroad” program, which means the China Scholarship Council (CSC) provides the living cost and travel expenses and the abroad graduate program or research supervisor must agree to cover tuition costs.

Data is a kind of valued strategic resources in the network era, yet mastering the data is insufficient. Instead, only with good command of data mining ability can I acquire the useful information inside it. Widely applied in business administration, production control, market analysis, engineering design and scientific exploration, data mining and analysis is closely bound up with our life. As for me, the undergraduate and postgraduate study in China has enormously sparked my interest in Machine Learning Systems and Big Data, as well allowed me to better understand the significance of adding value to data through the processing. In consequence, I wish to take PhD study, further deepening my knowledge structure and research ability and dedicating myself to the future development of data processing. Moreover, I firmly believe that I am a qualified and competitive applicant judging from my previous study and experience.

I have experienced in person the formidable power data carried on, which will eventually drastically change our life. However, a large number of professionals in this field are in highly demand in China to boost its future development. I wish to enhance my professional knowledge and hone practical skills in the PhD study, improving my competitiveness in a higher platform and better preparing me for future challenge.


Education

Academically, I obtained my BEng degree in Systems Engineering at National University of Defense Technology in 2014 and gained acceptance into postgraduate study here with top ranking among the department, waived all the entrance examinations. In the past years, my fervent interest and ambition spurred me to study in such an arduous way and made constant progress, and most of the major modules since the junior year have reached 90/100, which could be seen from my transcript. My academic excellence also won me several scholarships, which encouraged me to make greater efforts. In the master study, I am currently involved in the key project of National Natural Foundation of China, which focuses on organization modeling and calculation of social management in the context of network environment. It is closely linked with big data, which once again introduces me into the amazing world of data processing and makes me aware of its significance towards the whole society. The more I know about data processing, the deeper I want to probe into, thus I intend to study it further in PhD study.


Contests

Besides knowledge acquisition in classes, I also voluntarily participated in various mathematical modeling competitions, which not merely enhanced my thinking, essay writing and problem-solving abilities, but also in turn facilitated my academic studies. The topics covered optimization, complex system and data mining and often serving as the team leader, I was primarily responsible for essay writing and modeling. Our dedication eventually paid off as we won several prizes in the competitions. More importantly, not only did I reinforce my mathematical modeling capacity but also realize that a good state of mind and cooperation sense were of vital importance in study and research. Also delightfully, I tutored one of the younger mentees in our university, upgrading him from a new comer of this field to the outstanding winner of MCM for two consecutive years, which allowed me to enjoy great gratification from sharing my knowledge with others. Besides this, I also engaged in some innovative practice competition, in which I learned to detect problems, raise question and get familiar with the research methods, as well as drew on the precious experience from the excellent partners. I have also applied patents for some of my products made in these competitions.
